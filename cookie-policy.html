Cookie Policy

Transparency is key. SyncVeil uses minimal cookies required solely for authentication, security, and essential analytics.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the owners of the site. They allow a website to recognize your device and remember if you have been to the website before.

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while Session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.

2. Categories of Cookies We Use

We use both session and persistent cookies for the purposes set out below:

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for you to browse the website and use its features, such as accessing secure areas of the site. Without these cookies, services like the SyncVeil Vault cannot be provided.

  • AuthToken: Keeps you logged in securely.
  • CSRF-Token: Protects against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks.

Functionality Cookies

These allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your user name, language or the region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personal features.

  • ThemePref: Remembers if you prefer dark or light mode.
  • Language: Stores your preferred interface language.

Performance & Analytics

We use minimal, privacy-focused analytics to understand how our users interact with the platform (e.g., page load times, error rates). We do not track individual user behavior across other websites or use this data for advertising.

3. Third-Party Cookies

In some special cases, we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties. The following section details which third party cookies you might encounter through this site:

  • Payment Processing: If you use our premium features, our payment processor (e.g., Stripe) may set cookies to prevent fraud and ensure transaction security.
  • No Ad Networks: SyncVeil does not use third-party advertising cookies or share data with ad networks like Google Ads or Facebook Pixel.

4. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

If you prefer to avoid the use of cookies on the Website, first you must disable the use of cookies in your browser and then delete the cookies saved in your browser associated with this website. You may use this option for preventing the use of cookies at any time.

If you do not accept our cookies, you may experience some inconvenience in your use of the Website and some features (like the encrypted vault) may not function properly.
